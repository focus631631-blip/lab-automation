## Robert Kennedy (1865-1924) and the first surgical repair of neonatal brachial plexus palsy.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:45
- 期刊: Childs Nerv Syst
- 国家/单位: 05403-000 · University of São Paulo Medical School
- 通讯作者: Siqueira MG (mario.siqueira@hc.fm.usp.br)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00381-026-07375-w
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00381-026-07375-w) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42474776/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：回顾苏格兰外科医生Robert Kennedy的生平，并详细描述首次报告的新生儿臂丛神经产伤麻痹的手术修复案例。
- 核心发现/数据：Kennedy报告了最早的新生儿臂丛神经麻痹手术重建尝试，这些案例取得了显著良好的预后结果，且比现代神经外科手术时代早了几十年。
- 临床启示：该历史案例提示，早期外科干预可能对新生儿臂丛神经损伤有积极效果，为现代产伤麻痹的手术治疗提供了历史依据和借鉴。

**原文摘要**：This historical vignette highlights key aspects of the professional life of the Scottish surgeon Robert Kennedy and details the first reported surgical repair cases of brachial plexus birth palsy, which achieved remarkably favorable outcomes. These cases represent the first documented attempt at surgical reconstruction of neonatal brachial plexus palsy and predate the modern era of nerve surgery by several decades.

## Transanastomotic tube following surgery for congenital duodenal obstruction: its effect on nutrition and clinical outcomes.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:45
- 期刊: Pediatr Surg Int · 中科院医学3区(儿科3区/外科3区) · IF 1.8 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: Adana · Cukurova University Faculty of Medicine
- 通讯作者: Türker Çolak S (selcanturker@gmail.com)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06544-x
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06544-x) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42474529/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估先天性十二指肠梗阻术后应用跨吻合管（TAT）对新生儿营养支持、临床结局及并发症的影响。
- 核心发现/数据：TAT组开始肠内喂养时间（3.01±2.05天 vs 11.1±5.4天）和达到全肠内喂养时间（7.6±3.8天 vs 19.2±15.6天）均显著缩短；TPN使用时间（6.5±4.1天 vs 19.8±12.4天）及CVC置入率（23.8% vs 73.3%）显著降低；并发症发生率TAT组为19%，低于对照组的73.3%，且吻合口漏/狭窄仅发生在TAT(-)组。
- 临床启示：CDO术后放置TAT可促进早期肠内喂养，减少TPN和CVC依赖，降低并发症并缩短住院时间，尤其适用于高危新生儿术后营养管理。

**原文摘要**：Congenital duodenal obstruction (CDO) is a common neonatal intestinal obstruction associated with prematurity, low birth weight, and additional anomalies. Postoperative enteral feeding delays, caused by proximal duodenal dysmotility and anastomotic edema, prolong the need for total parenteral nutrition (TPN) and the use of a central venous catheter (CVC). The transanastomotic tube (TAT) bypasses the dysfunctional segment for direct jejunal feeding. This study evaluated effects of TAT on outcomes, TPN/CVC requirements, complications, and hospitalization duration in neonates undergoing CDO repair. Forty-eight CDO patients (January 2012-January 2021) were retrospectively reviewed. After excluding 7 pre-feeding deaths and 5 early TAT removals, 36 patients were analyzed: TAT(+) (n = 21) and TAT(-) (n = 15). Demographic characteristics, obstruction type, surgical technique, anomalies, feeding milestones, TPN duration, CVC use, complications, and hospitalization durations were compared. Groups were comparable for gestational age, birthweight, obstruction type, and associated anomalies. Time to initiation of enteral feeding was significantly shorter in TAT(+) group (3.01 ± 2.05 vs. 11.1 ± 5.4 days, p = 0.001), as was time to full enteral feeding (7.6 ± 3.8 vs. 19.2 ± 15.6 days, p = 0.002). TPN duration was shorter (6.5 ± 4.1 vs. 19.8 ± 12.4 days, p = 0.019) and CVC insertion rate lower (23.8% vs. 73.3%; p = 0.004) in TAT(+) group. Complication rates were 19% vs. 73.3% (p = 0.002); anastomotic leaks/strictures occurred exclusively in TAT(-) group. Hospitalization was shorter in TAT(+) patients (12.6 ± 4.9 vs. 25.2 ± 21.1 days, p = 0.03). TAT placement following CDO repair facilitates earlier enteral feeding, reduces TPN and CVC requirements, decreases complications, and shortens hospitalization. TAT is an effective postoperative nutritional strategy, particularly in high-risk neonates.

## Preoperative porta hepatis-to-hepatic surface ratio on 24-h delayed hepatobiliary scintigraphy is associated with jaundice clearance after Kasai portoenterostomy.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:45
- 期刊: Pediatr Surg Int · 中科院医学3区(儿科3区/外科3区) · IF 1.8 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: 日本 · Saitama Children's Medical Center
- 通讯作者: Deie K (pasers2006@gmail.com)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06550-z
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06550-z) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42474526/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估术前24小时延迟肝胆显像中肝门-肝表面比值与Kasai术后黄疸清除的关联性。
- 核心发现/数据：黄疸清除组患儿的24小时PH/HS比值显著高于未清除组（1.20 vs. 1.11，p=0.049），最佳截断值为1.15（内部验证AUC为0.670）；排除1年内移植患儿后，该比值与1年时胆红素水平呈负相关（r=-0.720），但与自体肝生存率及门脉板胆管直径相关性弱。
- 临床启示：PH/HS比值可作为Kasai术前无创预后指标辅助判断黄疸清除可能性，但当前结果尚属初步探索，需前瞻性多中心验证。

**原文摘要**：Biliary atresia (BA) often requires liver transplantation despite Kasai portoenterostomy (KPE). The prognostic role of hepatobiliary scintigraphy remains unclear. Twenty-three infants undergoing KPE between 2017 and 2024 were retrospectively analyzed. Patients without 6-h bowel activity on technetium-99 m PMT hepatobiliary scintigraphy underwent 24-h delayed imaging. The primary endpoint was jaundice clearance (total bilirubin < 2 mg/dL within 3 months). Ancillary analyses included an internally validated receiver operating characteristic (ROC) cutoff and correlation of the 24-h porta hepatis-to-hepatic surface (PH/HS) ratio with 1-year bilirubin, native liver survival (NLS), and portal plate bile duct diameter. Sixteen infants achieved jaundice clearance, and their 24-h PH/HS ratio was higher (1.20 vs. 1.11, p = 0.049). ROC analysis identified an exploratory cutoff of 1.15 (apparent area under the curve [AUC], 0.768; internally validated AUC, 0.670). Excluding patients transplanted within 1 year, the PH/HS ratio correlated inversely with 1-year bilirubin (r =  - 0.720). NLS did not differ significantly between groups, and the association with portal plate bile duct diameter was weak. The 24-h PH/HS ratio was associated with post-KPE jaundice clearance. However, these findings remain preliminary and hypothesis-generating. As a noninvasive preoperative parameter beyond routine laboratory testing, the PH/HS ratio warrants prospective multicenter validation.

## Impact of a low-cost lamb anus Hirschsprung pull-through surgery model: results from a structured national workshop.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:45
- 期刊: Pediatr Surg Int · 中科院医学3区(儿科3区/外科3区) · IF 1.8 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: 马来西亚 · Universiti Malaya and University of Malaya Medical Centre
- 通讯作者: Tan YW (ywtan80@doctors.net.uk)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06523-2
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06523-2) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42474777/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估一种低成本羊肛门模型在结构化全国培训研讨会中对小儿外科医生进行先天性巨结肠Soave拖出术训练的效果。
- 核心发现/数据：17名学员和14名导师完成34次模拟手术，操作信心中位数从2.5提升至7.0（P<0.0005），粘膜下剥离、袖套管理和吻合环节信心提升最显著（均从2.0或3.0提升至8.0，P<0.001）；模型真实感和培训实用性评分均为9/10，3-6个月随访显示100%的学员认为培训提高了协助或执行手术的能力。
- 临床启示：该低成本、高仿真模型可有效提升手术操作信心并转化为临床实践行为改变，具有可扩展性和伦理优势，适合推广用于儿科外科培训。

**原文摘要**：A nationwide simulated Hirschsprung's Soave pull-through surgery (HPTS) workshop using a lamb model was set up to train pediatric surgeons. Operative confidence, skills acquisition and clinical application were evaluated. The model (< 5 USD) comprised fresh lamb anorectum with terminal perianal muscle and skin preserved and a 20-cm rectum, inserted and fixed onto a mini basket, which allowed dissection using monopolar diathermy. Each trainee performed the five-step Soave procedure twice under supervision. Operative confidence for each step was measured pre- and post-workshop using a 10-point Likert scale, with model realism and utility similarly rated. A 3-6-month follow-up survey assessed clinical translation. Seventeen participants and 14 trainers from nine centres facilitated completion of 34 simulated procedures. Median overall confidence improved from 2.5 to 7.0 (P < 0.0005), with the greatest gains obtained in submucosal dissection (2.0 to 8.0), cuff management (2.0 to 8.0) and anastomosis (3.0 to 8.0) (all P < 0.001). Model realism and training utility were rated 9/10 and 9/10, respectively, by 25 respondents (93% response rate). Follow-up survey (12/17; 71%) showed 100% agreement that the workshop improved ability to assist or perform HPTS. This structured simulation-based HPTS workshop, using a validated low-cost model, significantly improved procedural confidence which translated into behavioural change in clinical practice. The model offers high fidelity, scalability and ethical training.

## Reduced KLF4 expression in endothelial cells triggers pulmonary vascular abnormalities associated with experimental congenital diaphragmatic hernia.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:45
- 期刊: Cell Mol Life Sci
- 国家/单位: 荷兰 · Erasmus MC - Sophia Children's Hospital
- 通讯作者: Rottier RJ (r.rottier@erasmusmc.nl)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00018-026-06319-6
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00018-026-06319-6) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42474740/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：探究先天性膈疝（CDH）模型中肺血管异常的早期分子机制，特别是内皮细胞中KLF4表达降低的作用。
- 核心发现/数据：在硝基fen诱导的CDH小鼠模型中，转录组分析显示内皮细胞与周细胞间的相互作用受到干扰；内皮细胞中Krüppel样因子4（Klf4）基因表达显著降低，且NOTCH信号通路成员表达异常，提示该通路在CDH中失调。
- 临床启示：KLF4在肺血管发育中发挥关键作用，其表达降低可能是CDH相关肺高压的早期病理基础，为产前干预或靶向治疗提供了新方向。

**原文摘要**：Congenital diaphragmatic hernia (CDH) is a rare anomaly with an incidence of 1 in 3000 live births and characterized by defective closure of the diaphragm, lung hypoplasia, and pulmonary vascular remodeling. Although several genes associated with the occurrence of CDH, no clear genetic component has been identified. Previously, we showed that CDH patients have vascular abnormalities already early in development, and that perivascular cells are affected in CDH. In the current study, we focused on early abnormalities in the nitrofen-induced CDH mouse model. Transcriptome analysis of FACS-sorted perivascular and endothelial cell populations from lungs of control and experimental mouse CDH revealed a high similarity in GO terms of differentially expressed genes (DEGs) between these populations, suggesting a disturbed interaction between endothelial cells and pericytes. The disturbed interaction is the basis for the aberrant development of the pulmonary vasculature in CDH. Furthermore, the RNA sequence data revealed reduced expression of the Kruppel like factor 4 (Klf4) gene in endothelial cells of CDH, which was confirmed with protein expression analysis. Furthermore, we show that KLF4 is an important up stream regulator of genes associated with vascular development and confirm that members of the NOTCH signaling pathway are differentially expressed, indicating that NOTCH signaling is disturbed in CDH. Collectively, our data support the importance of KLF4 in pulmonary angiogenesis and contribute to our previous data that pulmonary vessels in CDH patients are already affected before birth, which in turn may impact therapeutic strategies to reduce pulmonary hypertension associated with CDH.
