## Incidence and risk factors of postoperative delirium in children with congenital heart disease.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:28
- 期刊: PLoS One · 中科院综合性期刊3区(综合性期刊3区) · IF 2.8 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: 中国 · The First People's Hospital of Zunyi (The Third Affiliated Hospital of Zunyi Medical University)
- 通讯作者: Zhou MQ
- DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0329716
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0329716) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42467607/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：探讨接受先天性心脏病手术的儿童患者术后谵妄的发生率及其危险因素。
- 核心发现/数据：在187,272例CHD手术患儿中，POD确诊3,857例（发生率2.1%）。POD患儿合并症负担更高，总医疗费用增加，住院时间延长，院内死亡率升高（P<0.001），独立预测因素包括凝血功能障碍、水电解质紊乱和原有瘫痪。
- 临床启示：尽管儿童CHD术后POD发生率相对较低，但识别其易感因素对优化患儿围手术期管理和改善预后具有临床价值。

**原文摘要**：Postoperative delirium (POD) is an important neurocognitive complication following congenital heart disease (CHD) surgery in pediatric patients. While recognized for its association with adverse clinical outcomes, comprehensive population-based studies examining POD incidence and risk factors in this vulnerable population remain scarce. Using the Nationwide Inpatient Sample (NIS) database (2010-2019), a retrospective study was performed of pediatric patients (<18 years) undergoing CHD-related surgeries. We evaluated demographics (race, sex, age), hospital characteristics (region of the hospital, type of admission, bed size of hospital, teaching status of hospital, type of insurance, location of hospital), mortality, length of stay (LOS), total charges, perioperative complications, and comorbidities. Among 187,272 CHD surgery patients identified, POD was diagnosed in 3,857 (incidence 2.1%). Children with POD exhibited higher comorbidity burdens, increased total medical costs, prolonged LOS and increased rates of in-hospital mortality (P < 0.001). Independent predictors of POD included coagulopathy, fluid and electrolyte disorders, and preexisting paralysis. Additionally, POD was associated with in-hospital complications, including prolonged mechanical ventilation, acute renal failure, acute myocardial infarction, pneumonia, and respiratory failure. Although the incidence of POD following CHD surgery in children is relatively low, investigating its predisposing factors is clinically valuable for optimizing patient management and improving outcomes.

## Emergency repair of neonatal frontoethmoidal encephalocele: controlled mobilization of neonatal frontal bone flaps.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:28
- 期刊: Childs Nerv Syst
- 国家/单位: Erzurum · Atatürk University
- 通讯作者: Elveren M (dr.muhammetelveren@gmail.com)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00381-026-07400-y
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00381-026-07400-y) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42467257/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：探讨新生儿期额筛脑膨出（FEEM）急诊手术中，利用额骨瓣弹性可控移动技术获得充分手术暴露的可行性。
- 核心发现/数据：一名37周孕龄女婴出生第1天即发生活动性脑脊液漏，需紧急手术；采用双额开颅并利用新生儿骨弹性、未切割额骨瓣即可充分暴露颅底缺损，术后虽出现进行性脑积水需行脑室-腹腔分流，但伤口愈合良好且外观满意。
- 临床启示：对需急诊修复的新生儿额筛脑膨出，利用额骨瓣弹性进行可控移动可减少骨片分割需求，同时提供充足手术暴露。

**原文摘要**：Frontoethmoidal encephalomeningocele (FEEM) is a rare congenital malformation characterized by herniation of intracranial structures through a defect in the anterior skull base. Surgical management in the neonatal period is particularly challenging due to physiological fragility, limited surgical exposure, and the risk of cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) leakage and infection. We report a female neonate born at 37 weeks of gestation with a large FEEM involving the anterior horns of the lateral ventricles and frontobasal parenchyma herniating through the anterior skull base. Active CSF leakage developed on the first day of life, necessitating urgent surgical intervention. A bicoronal incision and bifrontal craniotomy with inferior frontal bone mobilization were performed. Taking advantage of the elasticity of neonatal bone, the frontal bone flaps were mobilized without segmentation, allowing adequate exposure of the defect. The postoperative course was uneventful, although progressive hydrocephalus required external ventricular drainage followed by ventriculoperitoneal shunt placement. At follow-up, wound healing was complete and the cosmetic outcome was satisfactory. This case highlights that controlled mobilization of elastic frontal bone flaps may provide adequate surgical exposure in neonatal patients requiring urgent repair and may reduce the need for additional bone segmentation.

## Pulse pressure as an early mortality predictor in neonates with congenital diaphragmatic hernia on veno-arterial ECMO.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:28
- 期刊: Pediatr Surg Int · 中科院医学3区(儿科3区/外科3区) · IF 1.8 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: 韩国 · University of Ulsan College of Medicine
- 通讯作者: Lee BS (mdleebs@amc.seoul.kr)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06529-w
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06529-w) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42467251/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估脉压作为接受静脉-动脉体外膜肺氧合治疗的先天性膈疝新生儿早期死亡预测指标的价值。
- 核心发现/数据：在33例新生儿中，存活组在ECMO后24小时（20.0 mmHg vs. 12.0 mmHg, p<0.001）和48小时（24.0 mmHg vs. 12.0 mmHg, p<0.001）的脉压显著高于死亡组；脉压预测存活的最佳临界值为24小时20 mmHg、48小时17 mmHg。
- 临床启示：早期脉压恢复可作为VA-ECMO支持下CDH新生儿生存的强预测指标，未来需研究增强脉压的干预措施能否改善该高危人群的临床结局。

**原文摘要**：Early prognostic markers for pediatric patients receiving extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO), particularly those with congenital diaphragmatic hernia (CDH), remain poorly defined. This study aimed to evaluate pulse pressure (PP) as an early hemodynamic indicator of mortality in neonates with CDH undergoing veno-arterial (VA) ECMO. We retrospectively analyzed newborns who underwent VA ECMO as primary rescue therapy for isolated CDH. Perinatal characteristics, arterial blood gas parameters, and blood pressure (BP) during the first 48 h post-ECMO were evaluated and compared between survivors and non-survivors. Among 33 neonates, 20 (60.6%) survived. ECMO flow rates or systolic, diastolic, and mean BP did not differ between the group. However, PP at 24 h (20.0 mmHg [17.8-26.3] vs. 12.0 mmHg [10.0-13.0], p < 0.001) and 48 h (24.0 mmHg [20.0-28.8] vs. 12.0 mmHg [10.0-14.0], p < 0.001) post-ECMO was significantly higher in survivors. Receiver operating characteristic analysis demonstrated that PP was am early predictor of survival, with optimal cutoff values of 20 mmHg at 24 h and 17 mmHg at 48 h. Early recovery of PP was a strong predictor of survival in neonates with CDH requiring VA-ECMO. Further research is required to determine whether interventions to enhance pulsatility can improve clinical outcomes in this high-risk population.

## Pregnancy After Uterine-Preserving Prolapse Surgery: Obstetric Outcomes and Prolapse Recurrence Following Native Tissue and Mesh-Augmented Repairs.

- 期刊: Int Urogynecol J · 中科院医学3区(妇产科学3区/泌尿学与肾脏学3区) · IF 2.0 · JCR Q3

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估保留子宫的盆腔器官脱垂（POP）手术后妊娠的产科结局及POP复发风险。  
- 核心发现/数据：纳入126例术后妊娠患者，64.3%接受自体组织修复，35.7%接受网片加固修复；足月分娩率达94.8%，剖宫产率为65.8%，无新生儿并发症报道；POP复发率为11.7%，其中71.4%发生在自体组织修复组。网片加固组（如骶骨子宫固定术）剖宫产率更高（85.7%），复发率更低。

**原文摘要**：To evaluate current literature on uterine-sparing surgery for pelvic organ prolapse (POP) with respect to subsequent obstetric outcomes and POP recurrence. A comprehensive search was conducted through MEDLINE and PubMed from 1993 to November 2025 using PRISMA guidelines for studies exploring patients who had subsequent deliveries after undergoing surgical management of POP. Articles were evaluated by three independent reviewers for quality, relevance, and outcomes assessment. Eighteen studies (six prospective, nine retrospective, and three case series) that included follow-up data on 126 patients with pregnancy after POP surgery were included in data analysis. The mean follow-up period was 37.2 months. The majority of patients had previously undergone native tissue POP repair, 64.3%, vs 35.7% who had undergone mesh-augmented repairs. Nearly all (94.8%) deliveries after POP surgery occurred at term. Cesarean delivery (CD) was common (65.8%). No neonatal complications were reported. POP recurrence rate was 11.7% with the majority (71.4%) occurring after native tissue repair. Analyzed separately, among 14 case reports of pregnancies following mesh-based sacrohysteropexy, cesarean delivery was common (85.7%) and prolapse recurrence rate was low. Current evidence suggests that uterine-sparing prolapse surgery, whether mesh-augmented or native tissue, is associated with favorable obstetric outcomes, low neonatal risk, and low rates of POP recurrence; however, longitudinal data is limited in the absence of randomized controlled trials.

## ARM in ARM? Investigating the co-occurrence of anorectal malformations and labioscrotal anomalies.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:28
- 期刊: Pediatr Surg Int · 中科院医学3区(儿科3区/外科3区) · IF 1.8 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: B15 2TT · Medical School
- 通讯作者: Sharman PGG (pgs000@student.bham.ac.uk)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06475-7
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06475-7) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42467260/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：系统探讨肛门直肠畸形（ARMs）与阴唇阴囊褶皱来源结构异常（LSAs）同时发生的相关性及模式。
- 核心发现/数据：在144篇符合标准的文献中，共报告319例同时患有ARM和LSA的病例。复杂型ARM患者中LSAs并发比例最高，其中58.4%仅伴有单一LSA；高位和低位ARM中，分别有66.0%和77.0%的病例仅伴随单一LSA。现有数据因缺乏LSA相关信息，难以根据ARM类型预测LSA存在，但本研究首次证实了ARM与LSA之间存在频繁关联。
- 临床启示：任何类型ARM患者均应尽早进行LSA筛查并及时处理，需小儿外科、泌尿科及其他临床医生密切协作。

**原文摘要**：Anorectal malformations (ARMs) are known to be associated with other defects, however there is no evidence addressing the co-occurrence of ARMs with anomalies of structures derived from the labioscrotal folds. We performed a systematic review (Prospero: CRD420251013945) aiming at investigating correlations and patterns between ARMs and labioscrotal anomalies (LSAs), when presenting simultaneously. Our search identified 1642 articles. After abstract screening, 344 full texts were assessed and 144 met the inclusion criteria. In total, these reported on 319 individual cases with both ARM and LSA. Individuals with complex ARMs had proportionally the most LSAs associated with their ARM - 58.4% of these were associated with only a single LSA. For 'high' and 'low' ARMs, 66.0% and 77.0% of cases were associated with a single LSA respectively. As data reporting prevalence of each type of ARM lacks information about LSAs, the potential to predict the presence of LSAs according to type of ARM is limited. Nevertheless, this review demonstrates a frequent association of ARMs with LSAs, that has not been previously described. This evidence highlights the importance of early screening and timely management of LSAs in patients with any type of ARM, with close collaboration between paediatric surgeons, urologists and other clinicians.

## Point-of-care ultrasound assessment of transrectal diameter to evaluate fecal load in children with anorectal malformations: adjunct imaging and utility in bowel management program.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:28
- 期刊: Pediatr Surg Int · 中科院医学3区(儿科3区/外科3区) · IF 1.8 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: Rostock · Pediatric Colorectal Centre
- 通讯作者: Lindert J (judith.lindert@med.uni-rostock.de)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06520-5
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06520-5) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42467098/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估床旁超声测量直肠横径作为无辐射辅助工具，在肛门直肠畸形儿童粪便负荷评估及排便管理监测中的价值。
- 核心发现/数据：无症状肛门直肠畸形患儿直肠横径值与健康对照组相当（2.20 vs. 2.05 cm，p=0.246），而有症状患儿直肠横径显著增大（3.64 cm，p<0.001）；受试者工作特征分析显示直肠横径截断值为3.1 cm时，灵敏度63.3%，特异度96.3%；在结构化排便管理中，直肠横径随临床改善降至该阈值以下。
- 临床启示：床旁超声测量直肠横径可作为肛门直肠畸形患儿临床评估的无辐射辅助手段，有助于在结构化排便管理方案中监测治疗反应，但不能替代整体结肠粪便负荷的放射学评估。

**原文摘要**：Children with anorectal malformations (ARM) frequently experience impaired bowel function and require long-term bowel management. Assessment of fecal load commonly includes abdominal radiographs, which provide information on overall stool distribution but may require repeated imaging and are subject to interobserver variability. Ultrasound measurement of the transrectal diameter (TRD) has been proposed as a radiation-free adjunct in functional constipation, but evidence in children with ARM remains limited. This study aimed to evaluate point-of-care ultrasound (POCUS) measurement of TRD as an adjunct to clinical assessment for evaluating fecal load and monitoring bowel management in children with ARM. In this prospective observational cohort study, children with ARM attending a tertiary colorectal clinic between April 2023 and January 2025 underwent standardized ultrasound assessment of TRD as part of a structured bowel management program. Findings were compared with children with functional constipation (FC) and healthy controls. TRD measurements were correlated with clinical symptoms of constipation and soiling. Receiver operating characteristic (ROC) analysis was performed. Fifty-seven children with ARM (mean age 5.7 years) were included, alongside 53 children with FC and 72 controls. Asymptomatic ARM patients demonstrated TRD values comparable to controls (2.20 vs. 2.05 cm, p = 0.246), whereas symptomatic ARM patients showed significantly increased TRD (3.64 cm, p < 0.001). ROC analysis identified a TRD cut-off of 3.1 cm with a sensitivity of 63.3% and specificity of 96.3%. During structured bowel management, TRD decreased below this threshold in parallel with clinical improvement. POCUS measurement of TRD is a feasible, radiation-free adjunct to clinical assessment in children with ARM. While it does not replace radiographic evaluation of global colonic stool burden, it may support monitoring within structured bowel management programs.

## Association of Invasive Intracranial Pressure Monitoring with Mortality in Severe Pediatric Traumatic Brain Injury: A National ACS TQIP Analysis.

- 来源: JPS 圣刊 · 推送 06:03
- 期刊: J Pediatr Surg · 中科院医学2区(儿科1区/外科2区) · IF 2.3 · JCR Q2

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估在严重儿童创伤性脑损伤中，中心级有创颅内压监测使用率与住院死亡率之间的关联。
- 核心发现/数据：在21,740名患儿中，29.6%（6,425例）接受了ICP监测；未监测组死亡率（20%）高于监测组（14%）（p<0.001）；每增加10%的中心级ICP监测使用率，住院死亡风险降低7%（OR 0.93, 95% CI 0.90-0.95, p<0.001）。

**原文摘要**：The impact of invasive intracranial pressure (ICP) monitoring in severe pediatric traumatic brain injury (TBI) remains uncertain, with variable practice patterns across trauma centers. Using the American College of Surgeons (ACS) Trauma Quality Improvement Program (TQIP), we evaluated whether centers with higher rates of ICP monitoring have improved patient outcomes. ACS TQIP data from 2013 to 2023 were analyzed for patients aged 2 to 18 with severe TBI. Exclusion criteria included being deceased on arrival, withdrawal of life-sustaining care, concomitant burns, and length of stay greater than 30 days. Data were aggregated based on admission year and center. Binomial generalized linear mixed models were used to assess adjusted associations between center-level ICP monitor use and in-hospital mortality. Of the 21,740 patients, 6,425 (29.6%) underwent ICP monitor placement. Despite more significant injury in the ICP monitor group, mortality was higher in patients without monitor placement (20% vs. 14%, p<0.001). After adjustment, increasing center-level ICP monitor use was associated with decreased mortality (OR 0.93, 95% CI 0.90-0.95, p<0.001), corresponding to a 7% reduction in the odds of in-hospital mortality for every 10% increase in ICP monitor utilization. In our nationwide evaluation of rates of ICP monitoring practices in pediatric patients with severe TBI, invasive ICP monitoring was associated with decreased mortality rates. Retrospective cohort study LEVEL OF EVIDENCE: III.

## Preventing Pediatric Backover Trauma: The Impact of a Federally Mandated Car Safety Feature.

- 来源: JPS 圣刊 · 推送 06:03
- 期刊: J Pediatr Surg · 中科院医学2区(儿科1区/外科2区) · IF 2.3 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: 美国 · McGovern Medical School at UTHealth Houston and Children's Memorial Hermann Hospital
- 通讯作者: Drucker NA (Natalie.a.drucker@uth.tmc.edu)
- DOI: 10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163303
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163303) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42468686/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估美国联邦强制要求新车配备后视摄像头（2018年5月起）对5岁以下儿童倒车碾压伤发生率和严重程度的影响。
- 核心发现/数据：创伤登记数据分析显示，强制令实施后儿童倒车碾压伤年发生率从11.6例降至5.3例（RR 0.46，95%CI 0.28-0.75），每10万儿童发病率从17.3降至7.1（Incidence RR 0.41，95%CI 0.27-0.60）；公共报告数据显示死亡病例减少78%（从18例降至4例），重伤比例亦下降。
- 临床启示：强制后视摄像头法规显著降低了儿童倒车碾压伤的发生率与致死率，临床医生应支持并宣传此类公共卫生法规，同时强调加强驾驶员对儿童盲区风险的意识教育。

**原文摘要**：Backover trauma occurs when a reversing motorized vehicle strikes a pedestrian, often a small child. Injuries carry significant morbidity and mortality. A federal mandate required newly manufactured motor vehicles meet a rear visibility standard by May 2018. This study characterizes the mandate's impact on pediatric backover trauma. A retrospective pre-post observational study was conducted of children (<5 years) with backover trauma (01/2011-11/2024). Two datasets were utilized: trauma registries from two Level I pediatric trauma centers serving a single catchment area, and case lists curated by Kids and Car Safety (KACS) from publicly available injury reports (same region). Univariate analyses and Poisson/quasi-Poisson models compared backover rates and incidences, pre-mandate versus post-mandate. From trauma registry data, 120 patients were identified: 85 pre-mandate, 35 post-mandate. Patients were median 2.1 years old (IQR:1.6-2.8), 65% male. Median ISS was 10.0 (IQR:5.0-17.3) with no difference between groups (p=0.68), but fewer severe injuries occurred post-mandate (12 pre-mandate, 5 post-mandate). Backover injury rate decreased from 11.6/year pre-mandate to 5.3/year post-mandate (RR:0.46; 95%CI:0.28-0.75), and incidence decreased from 17.3 to 7.1 per 100,000 (IncidenceRR:0.41; 95%CI:0.27-0.60). From KACS data, 28 victims were identified: 22 pre-mandate, 6 post-mandate. Backover injury rate was higher pre-mandate (3.0/year) and decreased post-mandate to 0.9/year (RR:0.30; 95%CI:0.12-0.75). Fatalities decreased from 18 (82%) pre-mandate to 4 (67%) post-mandate (p=0.58). Pediatric backover rates decreased following full compliance with the federal mandate. Fewer severe injuries occurred, and publicly reported fatalities decreased by 78%. Reduced rate, incidence, and severity of backover injuries justify public health-driven regulatory requirements.

## The Effect of Critical Congenital Heart Disease on Mortality and ECMO Utilization in Congenital Diaphragmatic Hernia: An Analysis of a Contemporary Cohort of Children's Hospitals.

- 国家/单位: 美国 · Yale School of Medicine

**原文摘要**：Hemodynamically significant patent ductus arteriosus (hsPDA) remains a major clinical challenge in preterm infants.
