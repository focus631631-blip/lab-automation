## Reducing barriers to pediatric surgical care: a cross-sectional study of the importance of a rural close-to-home clinic.

- 来源: JPS 圣刊 · 推送 01:44
- 期刊: J Pediatr Surg · 中科院医学2区(儿科1区/外科2区) · IF 2.3 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: 美国 · Center for Surgical Outcomes and Research
- 通讯作者: Mansfield SA (Sara.Mansfield@nationwidechildrens.org)
- DOI: 10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163299
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163299) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42431502/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估阿巴拉契亚地区农村家庭在前往中心化主院区与就近社区诊所接受儿科手术护理时所面临的就医障碍，并比较两种就诊方式的家庭体验。
- 核心发现/数据：在参与调查的276个家庭中（193个来自主院区，41个来自社区诊所），主院区家庭中92.2%需旅行超过1小时，而社区诊所家庭这一比例仅为37.5%（p<0.001）；主院区家庭中81.8%的旅行花费超过20美元，社区诊所家庭为41.7%（p<0.001）。社区诊所家庭在旅途舒适度、院内导航及寻找诊所的感知负担方面均显著降低（所有比较p≤0.003），且76.5%的家庭倾向于选择社区诊所就诊。
- 临床启示：在偏远地区设立就近社区诊所可显著减轻农村家庭就医的财务与后勤负担，改善就医体验，此类模式值得在类似农村地区推广。

**原文摘要**：Accessing pediatric surgical care is challenging for rural families given long travel distances, financial burden, and logistical barriers. Appalachia is a rural, socioeconomically distressed region with poor health outcomes. This study assessed barriers to care among Appalachian families attending appointments at a centralized Main Campus (MC) versus a decentralized Close to Home (CTH) clinic in Appalachia. Stakeholder-guided surveys were administered to families from Appalachian ZIP codes at MC (12/2023-9/2024) and a CTH pediatric surgery clinic in southeastern Ohio (11/2024-8/2025) upon check-in. Family experience and barriers to care, including distance traveled, travel time, financial strain, missed work/lost wages, and comfort with travel, parking, and locating the clinic, were compared by clinic location using chi-squared tests. Among 276 families approached, 79% participated (n=193 MC, n=41 CTH). Across both sites, most families traveled >1 hour (86.2%), spent >$20 on travel (77.3%), and missed work (70.1%) and wages (52.6%) to attend the appointment. Travel burden >1 hour (92.2% vs 37.5%, p< 0.001) and travel costs >$20 (81.8% vs 41.7%, p< 0.001) were more common among those attending MC than CTH appointments. Those attending the CTH clinic had significantly decreased perceived burden of accessing care including comfort in traveling to the appointment, navigating the campus, and finding the clinic (p≤0.003 for all comparisons). Overall, 76.5% preferred CTH clinic appointments, including 95.8% of CTH clinic attendees. Rural families seeking pediatric surgical care face notable logistical and financial barriers. A new Appalachian CTH clinic is well-received by families, significantly improving perceived accessibility. IV.

## NO PLACE LIKE HOME: DISCHARGE TIMING AND COSTS FOLLOWING PEDIATRIC GASTROSTOMY TUBE PLACEMENT IN THE UNITED STATES.

- 来源: JPS 圣刊 · 推送 01:44
- 期刊: J Pediatr Surg · 中科院医学2区(儿科1区/外科2区) · IF 2.3 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: 美国 · Ann & Robert H. Lurie Children's Hospital of Chicago
- 通讯作者: Lautz TB
- DOI: 10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163296
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163296) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42425443/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估美国儿童择期胃造口置管术后当日出院的应用趋势、医院间差异及其与调整后费用的关联。
- 核心发现/数据：在2016至2025年期间，来自46家医院的11,818例患儿中，仅9.8%实现当日出院；当日出院的中位费用为34,018美元，显著低于术后第1或第2天出院的49,491美元，调整后费用降低31%（费用比0.69，95% CI 0.58-0.82），相当于每例节省约17,000美元；当日出院比例从2016年的4.7%上升至2025年的12.0%，但实施不均，最高手术量的五家医院占据了全部当日出院病例的59%。
- 临床启示：尽管当日出院可显著降低费用，但其应用仍有限且医院间差异大，推广标准化的围手术期路径是提升儿科手术价值的重要机会。

**原文摘要**：Despite the proven safety of same-day discharge (SDD) after elective pediatric gastrostomy tube placement in appropriately selected patients, recent United States adoption trends remain unknown. Using a contemporary multicenter cohort, we evaluated the association between SDD and adjusted estimated costs as well as temporal trends and hospital-level variation in SDD. We conducted a retrospective cohort study of children ≤18 years undergoing elective gastrostomy tube placement between 2016 and 2025 using the Pediatric Health Information System database. Encounters with additional operative procedures were excluded. Discharge was dichotomized as SDD or discharge on postoperative day (POD) 1 or 2. Costs were estimated using generalized linear models, adjusting for patient demographics, medical complexity, illness severity, hospital volume, and year. Temporal trends and hospital-level variation in SDD were evaluated using logistic regression models. Among 11,818 children across 46 hospitals, SDD occurred in 1,155 encounters (9.8%). Median costs were lower for SDD than for discharge on POD 1 or 2 ($34,018 [IQR $17,959-$46,770] vs $49,491 [IQR $33,284-$71,159]). In adjusted analyses, SDD was associated with 31% lower costs (cost ratio 0.69, 95% CI 0.58-0.82), about $17,000 per encounter. SDD increased from 4.7% in 2016 to 12.0% in 2025, but adoption remained uneven: the five highest-volume hospitals accounted for 59% of all SDD cases. SDD after pediatric gastrostomy tube placement was associated with substantially lower costs, yet adoption remains limited and uneven across hospitals. Expanding standardized perioperative pathways to facilitate SDD represents an important opportunity to improve value in pediatric surgical care.

## Ultrasound Identification of Right Internal Jugular Vein Occlusion After Catheterization in a Premature Infant.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:44
- 期刊: Am J Case Rep · 中科院医学4区(医学：内科4区) · IF 0.8
- 国家/单位: 中国 · The Second People's Hospital of Huai'an/The Affiliated Huai'an Hospital of Xuzhou Medical University
- 通讯作者: Wang J
- DOI: 10.12659/AJCR.952818
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.12659/AJCR.952818) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42455772/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：报告一例早产儿右侧颈内静脉置管43天后发生完全闭塞的罕见病例，强调超声在识别血管并发症中的重要性。
- 核心发现/数据：该早产儿于2024年6月28日因肠梗阻手术行右侧颈内静脉置管，导管留置43天后于2024年10月22日再次需要中心静脉置管时，超声检查发现右侧颈内静脉完全闭塞。最终在超声引导下成功改为左侧颈内静脉置管。
- 临床启示：早产儿中心静脉置管后应警惕导管相关血栓及血管闭塞风险，超声作为一种无创、便捷的评估工具，应常规用于置管前后监测，尤其是有置管史的婴儿，以早期发现血管解剖异常并减少血管损伤。

**原文摘要**：BACKGROUND Right internal jugular vein (RIJV) catheterization is a routine clinical procedure for gastrointestinal surgery. The conventional blind puncture method is often unable to detect vascular anatomical variations, particularly in premature infants. Through widespread adoption of ultrasound-guided puncture techniques, the success rate of catheterization has substantially increased, and vascular anatomical abnormalities can be promptly identified. Internal jugular vein occlusion after catheterization is rarely reported in premature infants. This case report describes RIJV occlusion in a premature infant after 43 days of catheterization without preceding warning signs. CASE REPORT A premature infant underwent surgery for intestinal obstruction on June 28, 2024. A catheter was inserted into the RIJV and kept in place for 43 days. During catheterization, the catheter was managed according to standard nursing procedures. On October 22, 2024, central venous catheterization was required again. Ultrasound examination showed complete occlusion of the RIJV. Catheterization was successfully performed in the left internal jugular vein under ultrasound guidance as an alternative approach. CONCLUSIONS Several risk factors contribute to catheter-related thrombosis in premature infants undergoing central venous catheterization. Meticulous care measures should be implemented to maintain vessel patency after internal jugular vein catheterization. Early detection of thrombosis and occlusion is essential in infants. As a noninvasive and convenient assessment tool, ultrasound should be used to detect anatomical abnormalities and minimize vascular injury, particularly in infants with a history of catheterization.

## Single-stage versus staged reconstruction for high and intermediate anorectal malformations in neonates: a systematic review and meta-analysis.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:44
- 期刊: Pediatr Surg Int · 中科院医学3区(儿科3区/外科3区) · IF 1.8 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: Ho Chi Minh City · Vinmec International Hospital
- 通讯作者: Tran T (tuyen.tran97@gmail.com)
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06545-w
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06545-w) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42455200/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：比较新生儿一期与分期修复手术治疗中高位肛门直肠畸形的排便控制功能及感染结局。
- 核心发现/数据：纳入4项比较研究共746例患者（一期399例，分期347例），荟萃分析显示两组在排便失禁方面无显著差异（RR 0.93，95% CI: 0.52-1.66，p=0.80，95%预测区间0.28-3.08），但异质性高（I²=83%）；感染数据因报告方法学问题无法合并；所有研究存在中度至重度偏倚风险，GRADE证据质量：排便控制为低，感染为极低。
- 临床启示：目前低质量证据无法证实一期或分期修复在排便控制方面孰优孰劣，亦无法排除一期修复后更差结局的可能；亟需开展标准化结局的前瞻性研究指导决策。

**原文摘要**：The surgical approach to high and intermediate anorectal malformations remains controversial. This systematic review compared continence and infection outcomes between the two approaches. MEDLINE, Embase, and Cochrane CENTRAL were searched per a registered protocol (PROSPERO CRD420261291319). Comparative studies of single-stage versus staged repair in neonates with high or intermediate anorectal malformations were included. Risk of bias was assessed using ROBINS-I. Random-effects meta-analysis with prediction intervals was performed. Four comparative studies (746 patients: 399 single-stage, 347 staged) were included. Pooled analysis showed no significant difference in incontinence outcomes (RR 0.93, 95% CI: 0.52-1.66, p = 0.80; 95% prediction interval: 0.28-3.08) with substantial heterogeneity (I²=83%). Infection data could not be pooled due to methodological concerns in outcome reporting. All included studies demonstrated moderate to serious risk of bias. GRADE certainty was LOW for incontinence and VERY LOW for infection. Current evidence does not demonstrate superiority of either approach for bowel continence (LOW certainty). Infection evidence was insufficient for synthesis (VERY LOW). Wide prediction intervals indicate substantial uncertainty; available data cannot confirm or refute inferior outcomes after single-stage repair. Prospective studies with standardized outcomes are needed. Protocol registration: PROSPERO CRD420261291319.
