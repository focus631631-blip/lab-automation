## Is hypoalbuminemia a risk factor for small bowel anastomotic leaks in infants? A multivariate analysis.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:26
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06539-8
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06539-8) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42439979/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：探讨低白蛋白血症是否为婴儿小肠吻合口漏的危险因素。
- 核心发现/数据：在219例婴儿中，吻合口漏发生率为5.9%（13例）；发生吻合口漏的患儿术后血清白蛋白水平显著低于未发生者（20.3 g/l vs. 26.3 g/l，p < 0.0001），多变量分析证实低白蛋白水平与吻合口漏显著相关。
- 临床启示：术后低白蛋白血症可能是婴儿小肠吻合口漏的早期预警信号，但需进一步研究明确其是否为因果性危险因素。

**原文摘要**：Anastomotic leaks (AL) are critical complications following bowel anastomosis with an incidence of up to 10.5% in infants. Previous studies in animal models and adults have suggested a link between postoperative fluid overload and hypoalbuminemia with anastomotic leaks. In infants, the role of hypoalbuminemia on anastomotic integrity after small bowel anastomosis remains unclear. Retrospective study of infants < 1 year, undergoing elective small-bowel anastomosis between 2015 and 2024 (Ethics No.-11362-_BO_K_202). Data on demographics, weight gain, serum albumin levels and use of diuretic medication during the first five postoperative days were collected. Univariate and multivariate analysis were performed to detect risk factors for ALs in infants. 13 of 219 patients (5.9%) developed ALs. Demographics were similar comparing patients with and without leaks. In univariate analysis, serum albumin levels were significantly lower (20.3 g/l vs. 26,3 g/l, p < 0.0001) in patients with AL compared to patients without AL and multivariate analysis confirmed a significant association between low serum albumin levels and AL occurrence, CONCLUSION: In infants, small bowel anastomotic leaks are associated with low postoperative serum albumin. Whether hypoalbuminemia should be regarded as an early warning sign of an undiagnosed, developing anastomotic leak or represents a causal risk factor can only be determined with further studies.

## Long-term medical support after definitive surgery for esophageal atresia: analysis using a national inpatient database in Japan.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:26
- DOI: 10.1007/s00383-026-06538-9
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00383-026-06538-9) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42439942/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估食管闭锁根治术后患儿长期居家医疗与药物使用的真实世界模式。
- 核心发现/数据：806例患儿中，术后1年居家肠内营养是最常见的居家医疗方式（占22.0%），其比例随时间逐渐下降；抗酸药和促动力药的使用也随时间减少。合并先天性心脏病患儿需居家氧疗和肠内营养的比例显著高于无先天性心脏病患儿。
- 临床启示：食管闭锁根治术后有相当比例患儿需长期居家营养与呼吸支持，应针对合并先天性心脏病的高危人群加强术后随访与多学科管理。

**原文摘要**：Long-term medical needs after definitive surgery for esophageal atresia (EA) are incompletely understood. The real-world patterns of home medical care and medication use after hospital discharge remain underexplored. Using the Japanese national inpatient Diagnosis Procedure Combination database, we identified infants who underwent definitive surgery for EA between July 2010 and March 2023. We described patients' background characteristics, perioperative information, long-term home medical care (home tracheostomy care, home mechanical ventilation, home oxygen therapy, and home enteral nutrition), and medication use (antacids, prokinetic agents, and anti-asthmatics) at 1, 3, 5, and 7 years post-definitive surgery. Subgroup analysis based on congenital heart disease (CHD) was conducted. Overall, 806 patients met the eligibility criteria. Home enteral nutrition was the most frequently performed home medical care (22.0% of patients at 1 year post-surgery), whose proportion decreased gradually over time. Antacid and prokinetic agent use also declined over time. Subgroup analysis revealed that patients with CHD required home oxygen therapy and home enteral nutrition more frequently than those without CHD. A substantial proportion of patients required home longitudinal nutritional and respiratory support post-definitive surgery for EA. Our findings provide important information for long-term healthcare needs of patients receiving definitive surgery for EA. III (retrospective comparative study).

## The Forgotten Fetal Channel: Guidewire Entry Into Umbilical Vein During Central Line Placement in a Neonate.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:26
- DOI: 10.1213/XAA.0000000000002246
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1213/XAA.0000000000002246) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42439443/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：报告一例新生儿中心静脉置管过程中，导丝意外经脐静脉穿出的罕见病例，强调胎儿期血管结构持续存在对导管路径的影响。
- 核心发现/数据：一名体重1.8kg、因巨大腹膜后肿物引起腹腔间隔室综合征的4天男婴，在开放腹腔下行超声引导颈内静脉置管时，导丝经脐静脉穿出，提示存在动脉导管静脉（patent ductus venosus）这一胎儿期血管未闭的异常通道。
- 临床启示：新生儿可存在胎儿循环结构的持续开放（如动脉导管静脉），改变预期导管走行路径，需结合实时超声及透视引导，警惕导管误置风险。

**原文摘要**：Central venous catheterization in neonates presents unique challenges due to transitional circulatory physiology and potential persistence of fetal vascular structures. We describe a rare intraoperative observation in a 4-day-old baby boy weighing 1.8 kg, diagnosed with a large retroperitoneal mass causing abdominal compartment syndrome. The patient underwent emergency exploratory laparotomy. During ultrasound-guided internal jugular vein catheterization in the setting of an open abdomen, the guidewire was unexpectedly visualized emerging through the umbilical vein. This unusual finding indicated the presence of a patent ductus venosus, a fetal vessel that normally closes soon after birth but may remain patent for days or weeks in some neonates. The patent ductus venosus provided an anomalous communication between the umbilical and systemic venous systems, creating a potential pathway for guidewire misdirection. This case highlights the importance of understanding neonatal vascular anatomy, especially the persistence of fetal circulatory structures that may alter expected catheter trajectories. Awareness of such variations, coupled with real-time ultrasound and fluoroscopic confirmation, is essential to prevent inadvertent catheter misplacement and associated complications. This observation reinforces the need for heightened vigilance and imaging guidance during central venous access in critically ill neonates with altered hemodynamics and developmental vascular anomalies.

## An interpretable machine learning model for outpatient referral risk assessment in pediatric biliary atresia after Kasai portoenterostomy.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 01:26
- DOI: 10.1002/jpn3.70505
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1002/jpn3.70505) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42439215/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：开发基于常规门诊数据的可解释机器学习模型，用于辅助Kasai术后胆道闭锁患儿的风险分层和转诊评估。
- 核心发现/数据：在多中心回顾性研究中纳入512例III型胆道闭锁患儿，从8个儿科中心的门诊随访数据中筛选出10个关键变量；K近邻模型在测试集中表现最优（ROC曲线下面积0.954），校准度和临床效用良好；主要贡献变量包括白蛋白、胆汁淤积指数、静脉曲张预测规则和总胆汁酸。
- 临床启示：该模型为KPE术后门诊评估提供了便捷工具，可帮助临床医生识别高风险患儿并优化随访期间的风险分层。

**原文摘要**：To develop an interpretable model using routine outpatient data to assist risk stratification and referral assessment in children with biliary atresia (BA) after Kasai portoenterostomy (KPE). This multicenter retrospective study included 512 children with type III BA who underwent KPE across eight pediatric centers. Clinical, laboratory, and ultrasonographic variables collected during outpatient follow-up were analyzed. Feature selection was performed using least absolute shrinkage and selection operator, and eight machine learning algorithms were evaluated. Model performance was assessed using discrimination, calibration, and decision curve analysis. The optimal model was further interpreted using Shapley Additive Explanations analysis and implemented as an online calculator. Ten variables were selected for model development. Among the evaluated algorithms, the K-nearest neighbors model showed the best overall performance in the test set (area under the receiver operating characteristic curve 0.954) with good calibration and clinical utility. Key contributors included albumin, cholestasis index, the variceal prediction rule, and total bile acids. This model provides an accessible tool for outpatient assessment after KPE and may assist clinicians in identifying high-risk patients and improving risk stratification during follow-up.

- DOI: 10.1007/s00464-026-13124-6

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估经阴道自然腔道内镜手术（vNOTES）治疗良性妇科疾病后的妊娠结局，并探讨术后至受孕的时间间隔对不良结局风险的影响。
- 核心发现/数据：术后妊娠组与无手术史对照组在分娩方式、胎龄、新生儿出生体重及母体并发症方面无显著差异；双胎妊娠是早产（HR 11.736，p<0.001）和新生儿窒息（HR 29.302，p=0.019）的独立危险因素，而vNOTES术后至受孕的间隔时间（≤3个月、3-6个月、>6个月）与不良结局无显著关联。
- 临床启示：vNOTES是一种安全的保留生育功能的手术，术后无需长期推迟受孕，早期妊娠不增加不良产科结局风险。

**原文摘要**：Transvaginal natural orifice transluminal endoscopic surgery (vNOTES) is increasingly used for benign gynecologic conditions; however, evidence regarding subsequent obstetrical outcomes and the optimal interval between surgery and conception remains limited. This study aimed to evaluate pregnancy outcomes after fertility-preserving vNOTES and to assess whether the postoperative interval influences the risk of adverse outcomes. This prospective cohort study included 326 women who conceived after fertility-preserving vNOTES performed for benign gynecological diseases with cosmetic considerations between April 2022 and June 2024, along with 326 matched controls without a history of vNOTES during the same period. Post-vNOTES pregnancies were stratified by the interval from surgery to conception (≤ 3, 3-6, and > 6 months). Obstetrical outcomes, including delivery mode, gestational age, neonatal birth weight, maternal complications, preterm birth, and neonatal asphyxia, were compared. Multivariable logistic regression was performed to identify independent risk factors for preterm birth and neonatal asphyxia. Baseline characteristics were generally comparable, although the control group had higher parity and more prior cesarean deliveries (both p < 0.001). The distribution of surgical indications differed across interval groups (p < 0.001). The vNOTES group had higher rates of assisted reproductive technology and twin pregnancies (both p < 0.001). No significant differences were observed between groups in delivery mode, gestational age at birth, neonatal birth weight, or maternal complications. In regression analyses, twin pregnancy was independently associated with preterm birth (HR 11.736, p < 0.001) and neonatal asphyxia (HR 29.302, p = 0.019), while lower gestational age was also associated with neonatal asphyxia (HR 0.863, p = 0.018). The interval between vNOTES and conception was not associated with adverse outcomes. Pregnancy outcomes following vNOTES are comparable to those without prior vNOTES. Early conception after vNOTES does not appear to increase the risk of adverse obstetrical outcomes, supporting vNOTES as a safe fertility-preserving surgical approach without the need for prolonged delay before conception. ## Outcomes after primary surgical repair of coarctation of the aorta through lateral thoracotomy.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 06:10
- DOI: 10.1016/j.rec.2026.06.012
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rec.2026.06.012) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42442609/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：评估经左胸外侧切口行主动脉缩窄一期手术修复的远期结局，并分析不良事件的风险因素。
- 核心发现/数据：共纳入401例患者，中位随访10.3年，20年总体生存率超过98%；再手术率4.5%，导管再干预率9.7%，中位时间均约10年。室间隔缺损、合并心脏畸形、补片成形术与死亡率增加相关；修复时年龄较大、早产、主动脉瓣下狭窄、假体植入及合并心脏缺陷独立预测再手术，而主动脉弓发育不良及新生儿期修复增加导管再干预风险。
- 临床启示：经左胸切口修复主动脉缩窄的远期生存率极佳，但晚期不良事件受解剖复杂性和患者因素影响，强调需要结构化的终身随访。

**原文摘要**：Simple coarctation of the aorta (CoA) can be repaired through left lateral thoracotomy without cardiopulmonary bypass. However, late adverse events such as death, restenosis, aneurysm formation, and development of hypertension can occur, even after initial successful repair. In this study, we evaluated outcomes after surgical repair of CoA through left lateral thoracotomy and examined risk factors for adverse events. Among 986 patients who underwent CoA repair at our institution from 2001 to 2023, we selected patients repaired through left lateral thoracotomy and excluded those with univentricular circulation. Surgical outcomes were evaluated using cumulative incidence curves, and risk factors were analyzed using Cox regression models. A total of 401 patients were included; 138 (34.4%) were female and 56.6% had isolated CoA. Median age at surgery was 30 days. During a median follow-up of 10.3 years (interquartile range, 3.3-17.8 years), overall survival exceeded 98% at 20 years. Reoperation was required in 4.5% of patients and catheter-based reintervention in 9.7%, both after a median of approximately 10 years. Ventricular septal defect, associated heart defects, and patch plasty were associated with increased mortality. Older age at repair, prematurity, subaortic stenosis, prosthesis implantation, and associated cardiac anomalies independently predicted reoperation, while hypoplastic aortic arch and repair in the neonatal period increased the risk of catheter-based reintervention. Survival after lateral thoracotomy repair of aortic coarctation is excellent. However, late adverse events are influenced by anatomical complexity and patient-related factors, underscoring the need for structured lifelong follow-up.

## Characteristics and environmental susceptibility of first-pass meconium microbiota in neonates with congenital intestinal atresia.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 06:10
- DOI: 10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163290
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163290) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42442462/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：探究先天性肠道闭锁是否因宫内肠道环境改变而特异性地影响新生儿胎便微生物群特征。
- 核心发现/数据：先天性肠道闭锁组新生儿胎便的α多样性（Shannon指数和观察特征）在所有组中最低，尤其在剖宫产或母亲抗生素暴露时显著降低（p<0.05）；β多样性分析显示闭锁组与健康对照组微生物分布存在显著差异（p<0.05）。在门水平，闭锁组拟杆菌门比例升高，而厚壁菌门和变形菌门减少；属水平上，闭锁组假单胞菌属增加，葡萄球菌属（vs.对照组及其他畸形组，p<0.05）和大肠埃希菌属（vs.其他畸形组，p<0.05）显著减少。
- 临床启示：先天性肠道闭锁新生儿胎便微生物群具有独特特征，提示其胎儿期肠内环境与正常及其他先天畸形新生儿不同，可能为产前诊断或早期干预提供潜在生物标志物。

**原文摘要**：Whether congenital gastrointestinal atresia (atresia) specifically affects the meconium microbiota because of an altered intrauterine intestinal environment remains unclear. Therefore, we aimed to characterize the meconium microbiota of neonates with congenital anomalies, specifically atresia. Meconium samples were collected from healthy term neonates (control), neonates with congenital malformations other than atresia (other anomalies), and those with atresia who were admitted to the growing care unit or obstetrics ward. Alpha-diversity (Shannon index and observed features) and beta-diversity (principal coordinate analysis) were assessed through 16S rRNA gene sequencing. The microbial composition was examined at the phylum and genus levels, and stratified by delivery mode and mother's antibiotic exposure. The study included 20 controls, 37 neonates with other anomalies, and 11 with atresia, including esophageal, duodenal, small intestinal, and colonic atresia. Alpha-diversity was the lowest in atresia, particularly with cesarean delivery or maternal antibiotic exposure (p<0.05). Beta-diversity analysis demonstrated that the distribution of microbial profiles significantly differed between the control and atresia groups (p<0.05). At the phylum level, atresia had a high proportion of Bacteroidetes, whereas Firmicutes and Proteobacteria were reduced. Several genera that were abundant in the control were markedly reduced in atresia (p<0.001 vs. control; p<0.01, among 3 groups), with higher Pseudomonas but lower Staphylococcus (p<0.05, vs. control and other anomalies) and Escherichia (p<0.05 vs. other anomalies). Congenital gastrointestinal atresia demonstrated unique meconium microbiota profiles compared with those of healthy neonates and other congenital anomalies. This suggests differences in the intraintestinal environment during the fetal period. Ⅱ.

## Spontaneous gastric perforation in a 12-year-old adolescent girl: a case report and literature review.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 06:10
- DOI: 10.1186/s12887-026-07306-3
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1186/s12887-026-07306-3) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42443798/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：报告一例12岁女孩自发性胃穿孔的诊疗经过并复习文献，总结该病在年长儿童及青少年中的临床特征、诊断与治疗策略。

- 核心发现/数据：患儿表现为突发上腹剧痛、强迫体位、恶心呕吐，腹平片示双膈下游离气体，急诊腹腔镜探查发现胃窦后壁近幽门处约0.5 cm×0.5 cm圆形穿孔，行腹腔镜双层缝合修补术后恢复良好。文献复习表明自发性胃穿孔在年长儿中罕见，且后壁幽门前区穿孔位置隐匿易致漏诊。

- 临床启示：年长儿童急性腹痛鉴别诊断应考虑自发性胃穿孔；对病程短、生命体征稳定、腹腔污染局限的患儿，腹腔镜探查可明确穿孔部位并同期实施微创修补，早期识别与个体化手术决策是改善预后的关键。

**原文摘要**：Spontaneous gastric perforation (SGP) is a rare but life-threatening acute abdominal emergency. It is more commonly reported in neonates, whereas cases in older children and adolescents are uncommon. Because of its nonspecific clinical manifestations, SGP is prone to misdiagnosis or delayed treatment. We report a successfully treated case of SGP in a 12-year-old girl, with the perforation located in the relatively concealed posterior prepyloric region of the stomach. Combined with a literature review, this report discusses the clinical features, potential pathogenesis, imaging findings, and diagnostic and therapeutic strategies of pediatric SGP, and evaluates the clinical value of laparoscopic exploration in early diagnosis and treatment. A 12-year-old adolescent girl was admitted with a 5-h history of abdominal pain. She presented with sudden severe epigastric pain, a forced posture, nausea, and vomiting. Abdominal radiography showed a large amount of free gas beneath both hemidiaphragms, suggesting gastrointestinal perforation. Abdominal computed tomography further supported the preliminary diagnosis of gastric perforation with acute peritonitis. Emergency laparoscopic exploration revealed an approximately 0.5 cm × 0.5 cm round perforation in the posterior prepyloric wall of the stomach, with no obvious abnormalities in the remaining gastric wall. Laparoscopic two-layer primary suture repair was performed. Postoperatively, the patient received anti-infective and supportive treatment, recovered well, and experienced no related complications during follow-up. Although SGP is rare in older children and adolescents, it should be included in the differential diagnosis of pediatric acute abdomen. Perforation of the posterior prepyloric wall is relatively concealed, and early clinical manifestations and inflammatory responses may be atypical, increasing the risk of missed diagnosis. Timely imaging evaluation is essential for early diagnosis. In children with a short disease course, stable vital signs, limited intra-abdominal contamination, and a small perforation, laparoscopic exploration can help identify the perforation site and assess the degree of contamination, while allowing simultaneous minimally invasive repair. This case suggests that early recognition, prompt imaging assessment, and individualized surgical decision-making are key to improving clinical outcomes in pediatric SGP.

## Predicting Neonatal Respiratory Outcomes Using Machine Learning: A Systematic Review Through a Life Course Lens.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 06:10
- DOI: 10.1097/ANC.0000000000001392
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1097/ANC.0000000000001392) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42441483/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：通过系统综述，评估基于人工智能/机器学习模型预测新生儿呼吸结局的现状，并从“生命历程健康发展”视角分析模型是否纳入发育时机、累积过程及背景因素。
- 核心发现/数据：在4319篇初始文献中，16项研究最终入选（0.37%），主要涉及支气管肺发育不良（8项）、呼吸窘迫综合征（4项）等；多数模型在内部验证中表现出强区分能力（受试者工作特征曲线下面积≥0.85），但外部验证极少（仅2/16），校准报告稀疏，且无研究预测出院后呼吸结局或纳入全面健康决定因素。
- 临床启示：机器学习模型在新生儿重症监护病房风险分层中具有潜力，但负责任的临床采用需开展多中心外部验证、常规校准报告，并纳入基于护理背景的发育相关设计。

**原文摘要**：Neonatal respiratory outcomes remain leading drivers of neonatal intensive care unit (NICU) morbidity, mortality, and prolonged hospitalization, underscoring the need for accurate early risk prediction through artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) approaches. To conduct a Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses-guided systematic review synthesizing AI/ML models predicting neonatal respiratory outcomes through a Life Course Health Development (LCHD) lens, examining whether models incorporate developmental timing, cumulative processes, and contextual factors. Included peer-reviewed studies developing or validating AI/ML prediction models for neonatal respiratory outcomes with performance metrics. Two reviewers independently extracted study characteristics, inputs, algorithms, validation strategies, and performance metrics. Risk of bias was assessed using PROBAST + AI. Disagreements were resolved by consensus. Discrimination was strong in internal validation (several models with an area under the receiver operating characteristic curve ≥0.85). External validation was rare (2/16); calibration reporting was sparse. No studies predicted postdischarge respiratory outcomes or included comprehensive determinants of health. Although ML models show promise for NICU risk stratification, responsible clinical adoption requires multisite external validation, routine calibration, and nursing-informed designs incorporating development context.
