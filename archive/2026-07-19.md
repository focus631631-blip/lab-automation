## Vacuum-Assisted Mini-PCNL Versus RIRS with flexible and navigable suction ureteral access sheath for 1-3 cm Pediatric Stones: A Comparative Study.

- 来源: JPS 圣刊 · 推送 06:28
- 期刊: J Pediatr Surg · 中科院医学2区(儿科1区/外科2区) · IF 2.3 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: 中国 · The Fifth Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou Medical University
- 通讯作者: Yang W (vgyang@qq.com)
- DOI: 10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163313
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163313) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42471090/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：比较真空辅助微通道经皮肾镜取石术（VA-mini PCNL）与可弯曲负压吸引输尿管通道鞘辅助的逆行肾内手术（FANS-RIRS）治疗1-3 cm儿童上尿路结石的效果。
- 核心发现/数据：两组清石率（97.3% vs. 91.3%，p=0.552）、手术时间（95 vs. 85分钟，p=0.398）及并发症率（2.7% vs. 4.35%，p=1）无显著差异；VA-mini PCNL住院时间更长（9 vs. 7天，p=0.001），但费用更低（1921.50美元 vs. 2339.68美元，p<0.001）。
- 临床启示：两种技术均是治疗1-3 cm儿童上尿路结石的可行方案，VA-mini PCNL更具成本效益，而FANS-RIRS可缩短住院时间。

**原文摘要**：Compare vacuum-assisted mini-percutaneous nephrolithotomy (VA-mini PCNL) and RIRS with flexible and navigable suction ureteral access sheath (FANS-RIRS) for pediatric upper urinary tract stones. This retrospective study included children under 14 years old with 1-3 cm stones treated from March 2021 to June 2024. Patients matched for demographics and stone characteristics. Primary outcomes included: stone-free rate (SFR), complications, lab parameters, operative time, hospitalization cost and duration. There were 37 children in the VA-mini PCNL group (16 Fr sheath) and 23 in the FANS-RIRS group (10/12 Fr sheath). In the FANS-RIRS group, 34.78% underwent preoperative stent placement. No significant differences were found between groups in SFR (97.3% vs. 91.3%, p=0.552), surgical time (95 vs. 85 minutes, p=0.398), or complication rate (2.7% vs. 4.35%, p=1). Complications included fever in VA-mini PCNL and perirenal hematoma in FANS-RIRS. Stone baskets were used only in FANS-RIRS (21.74%, p=0.006). The VA-mini PCNL group had an 81.1% tubeless rate. Neither surgery significantly affected creatinine, blood urea nitrogen (BUN), or hemoglobin levels (p>0.05). Postoperative white blood cell counts increased in both groups (p=0.003 and p=0.039, respectively), with no significant difference between groups (p=0.331). VA-mini PCNL had a longer hospital stay (9 vs. 7 days, p=0.001) but lower costs ($1921.50 vs. $2339.68) , p<0.001). Both techniques are viable options for pediatric upper urinary tract stones of 1-3 cm.VA-mini PCNL is more cost-effective, while FANS-RIRS offers a shorter hospital stay.

## Volvulus in preterm neonates: An overlooked diagnosis.

- 来源: JPS 圣刊 · 推送 06:28
- 期刊: J Pediatr Surg · 中科院医学2区(儿科1区/外科2区) · IF 2.3 · JCR Q2
- 国家/单位: London · Evelina London Children Hospital
- 通讯作者: Khodary AR (ahmedrefaatkhodary@gmail.com)
- DOI: 10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163314
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpedsurg.2026.163314) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42471089/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：比较早产儿与足月儿发生肠扭转的术前表现、诊断路径、手术结果及术后预后，以揭示早产儿肠扭转常被漏诊的现状。
- 核心发现/数据：早产儿出现胆汁性呕吐的比例显著低于足月儿（p=0.015），但腹胀（p=0.02）及全身状态恶化更常见，初始临床印象常误判为坏死性小肠结肠炎（NEC）；早产儿从转诊到手术的中位时间更长（12小时 vs. 2.5小时），肠道切除率更高（66.6% vs. 44.4%），且术后并发症显著增多（p<0.01），达到全量喂养时间更长（p=0.010），住院时间更长（p=0.007）。
- 临床启示：早产儿肠扭转因表现不典型常被误诊为NEC，导致手术延迟及更高的并发症和死亡率，临床医生应提高警惕，对腹胀、全身状况恶化的早产儿尽早考虑肠扭转可能。

**原文摘要**：Intestinal volvulus is a potentially devastating condition where prompt surgical management can avert significant mortality and morbidity. However, the diagnosis in preterm infants can be challenging. We aimed to compare preoperative presentation, diagnostic pathways, surgical management and postoperative outcomes between preterm and full-term infants with intestinal volvulus. All neonatal patients with intestinal volvulus confirmed on laparotomy in the two year period October 2023 to September 2025 were identified using the electronic patient record. Patients were categorized into Group A (preterm, <37 weeks) and Group B (full-term, ≥37 weeks). Data including demographics, age at presentation, reason for referral, time to surgery, and postoperative course were extracted from the electronic patient record and compared. Eighteen infants were included (9 per group). Premature infants were significantly less likely to present with bilious vomiting (p=0.015) and more likely to present with abdominal distension (p=0.02) and generalized deterioration. Initial clinical impression favoured necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC) in preterms. Preterm infants had a longer median time from referral to surgery (12 vs. 2.5 hours) and higher intestinal resection rates (66.6% vs. 44.4%), though these did not reach statistical significance. Preterm infants experienced significantly more postoperative complications (p<0.01), longer times to full feeds (p=0.010), and longer hospital stays (p=0.007). Volvulus in preterm infants is frequently misidentified as NEC due to atypical presentation. This diagnostic challenge leads to delays in surgical intervention and is associated with significantly higher morbidity and mortality compared to term infants.

## Critical ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome leading to severe malnutrition: shedding some lights on an extremely rare case report from Vietnam and narrative review.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 06:28
- 期刊: BMC Pregnancy Childbirth · 中科院医学2区(妇产科学2区) · IF 3.1 · JCR Q1
- 国家/单位: 越南 · Tu Du Hospital
- 通讯作者: Nguyen PN (docternhon@gmail.com)
- DOI: 10.1186/s12884-026-09603-2
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1186/s12884-026-09603-2) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42471618/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：报告一例因卵巢过度刺激综合征（OHSS）导致严重营养不良的罕见病例，并探讨其诊断与治疗策略。
- 核心发现/数据：一名34岁多囊内分泌代谢卵巢综合征（PMOS）患者，在促排卵过程中出现OHSS与自然妊娠共存，孕16周接受期待治疗后病情恶化，至孕25-26周因严重OHSS住院，出现无法进食、体重急剧下降、肌萎缩、贫血、电解质紊乱及肠动力障碍；经开腹手术减液及减瘤后症状缓解，但后续出现胎儿生长受限（FGR），最终于孕36周3天因严重FGR和胎盆不称行计划性剖宫产，母婴均顺利出院。
- 临床启示：对于PMOS患者促排卵早期应警惕OHSS与自然妊娠共存；当OHSS导致严重消化道并发症及营养不良时，多学科协作下的手术干预可能改善母婴结局，并需密切监测FGR。

**原文摘要**：Ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome (OHSS) is a self-limited iatrogenic complication of controlled-ovarian stimulation. The condition occurs spontaneously or secondary to exogenous gonadotropins which may cause a severe condition. Herein, we outline an extremely rare complication of a such entity leading to severe malnutrition. Additionally, the patient underwent resected surgery of ovarian hyperstimulated tissue in pregnancy. A 34-year-old nulliparous pregnant with a history of polyendocrine metabolic ovarian syndrome (PMOS) received an ovarian stimulation protocol at a private clinic setting. The patient was scheduled for in vitro fertilization (IVF) due to infertility during 15 months. However, on the day 4-5 of trigger, the patient was detected with co-existence of OHSS and spontaneous pregnancy. The pregnant woman was hospitalized at 25-26 weeks of gestational age (GA) for critical OHSS complicating in asthenia and malnutrition following the expectant management of OHSS at 16 weeks of GA. Ultrasound scan revealed multiple anechogenic enlarged cysts extending to the bilateral diaphragm and a great amount of intraabdominal fluid accumulation. During hospitalization, the woman was unable to feed herself, thus leading to severe loss of weight, amyotrophy, and anemia rapidly. Her condition was completely worsened. Seriously, a significant disturbance of bowel motility was noted. The electrocytes disturbance was remarkable. Serum albumin decreased gradually. Therefore, the patient underwent laparotomy to reduce the abdominal fluid and cytoreductive surgery owing to critical OHSS. After surgical intervention, the serum laboratory returned progressively to normal value and the clinical symptoms were steadily subsided. The patient was discharged and continued her pregnancy. Ultrasound showed a complete regression of the ovarian cysts; however, the fetal growth restriction (FGR) was recorded. The patient underwent a planned cesarean delivery at 36 weeks 3 days of GA due to severe FGR and fetal-pelvic disproportion. Both mother and neonate were discharged uneventfully. In the patient with PMOS, co-existence of OHSS and spontaneous pregnancy at the beginning of ovarian stimulation should be recognized. In general, a rapid progression of OHSS associated with adverse outcomes relating to gastro-intestinal complications are extremely scarce. FGR ought to be taken into account in a pregnancy with OHSS. Surgical intervention with a multidisciplinary management could improve materno-neonatal outcomes. Thus, this endeavor insight could be considered in critical cases with potentially life-threatening complications.

## "Necrotizing enterocolitis": a paradigm shift.

- 来源: PubMed · 推送 06:28
- 期刊: Pediatr Res · 中科院医学3区(儿科2区) · IF 3.3 · JCR Q1
- 国家/单位: 美国 · Wake Forest School of Medicine
- 通讯作者: Neu J (neuj@peds.ufl.edu)
- DOI: 10.1038/s41390-026-05303-x
- 获取全文: [DOI 全文](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41390-026-05303-x) · [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42471466/)

**中文总结**：- 研究目的：探讨新生儿坏死性小肠结肠炎（NEC）诊断分类的局限性，并提出以数据驱动、基于机制的聚类分类法替代传统单一“NEC”标签的必要性。  
- 核心发现/数据：传统诊断工具（如放射学标准和Bell分期）无法区分生物学上不同的肠损伤类型，导致治疗不当和研究混乱；通用生物标志物的寻找因缺乏机制清晰度而受阻；作者主张结合临床病程与多组学分析建立基于聚类的分类体系。  
- 临床启示：转向机制导向的精准分类有望实现个性化干预、优化临床试验设计，从而改善新生儿患者的治疗效果和预后。

**原文摘要**：Necrotizing enterocolitis (NEC) reveals substantial heterogeneity in presentation and underlying mechanisms. Conventional diagnostic tools, especially radiographic criteria and Bell staging, fail to distinguish between biologically distinct forms of intestinal injury, leading to imprecise management and research practices. The continued search for universal biomarkers is hindered by a lack of NEC mechanistic clarity. This commentary discusses abandoning the monolithic "NEC" label in favor of a data-driven, cluster-based taxonomy that integrates clinical trajectories with multiomic analyses. Such a shift would enable mechanism-informed diagnoses, personalized interventions, and more meaningful clinical trials, ultimately improving care and outcomes for vulnerable neonatal patients. IMPACT: NEC diagnosis lacks precision, leading to overtreatment and research confusion. Bell staging and biomarkers fail due to the heterogeneity of the diagnosis. Moving to data-driven, mechanism-based classification will enable targeted, effective care and better research outcomes.
